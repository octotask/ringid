FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS (FAQs)

 
REGISTRATION
What is ringID?
ringID is an all-in-one app that makes you versatile! It gives you all the social networking features in one place with Live, Channels, Video & Voice Calling, Fun Stickers, Messaging, Secret Chat, Music, News and much more for free. ringID is perfect for users who look for one dynamic app for all their social needs.
How do I use ringID from the website?
Installing ringID is very easy and can be done from any internet connected device using a web browser.
  • Open the browser
  • Go to the ringID website (https://www.ringid.com/)
  • Enter your mobile number and password
  • Click on "Sign In" and enjoy!
If you do not have a ringID account, then you can register with ringID by clicking on "Sign-up"
How do I sign up for ringID?
Registering as a new user to ringID is no hassle. You can easily register with ringID using your mobile phone number.
  • Open the ringID website: https://www.ringid.com/
  • To sign up as a new user, click on "Sign-up"
  • Enter your mobile phone number
  • Receive a verification code via SMS
  • Enter the verification code to create your unique ringID
  • Create a password and enjoy!
How do I use this app?
ringID is very easy to set up and use.
  • Create your profile and fill in details such as user info and add profile picture/cover photo
  • Post pictures, articles, status updates with a timer on your newsfeed
  • Make new friends and add friends on your network
  • Explore other features such as newsfeed, secret chat, media cloud, news portal, stickers & more
PROFILE
How do I go to my profile page?
You can enter your profile page by clicking on your profile picture which is always visible on the top left corner. Your name, ringID and online status are also visible at all times.
How to edit my Basic Info?
  • To edit your Basic Info go to your profile page
  • Click on the "About" thumbnail from your profile
  • Under "Update basic information", fill out details such as full name, gender, etc and click "Update"
  • Click on "Add" and fill out other details such as education, career and skills and later click "Save"
How do I add or change my Profile Picture?
  • First go to your profile page.
  • Hover over your Profile Picture and click on the camera icon.
  • Click on "Upload Image" to select a photo from your device.
  • Click on "Browse Albums" to choose from your ringID photos.
  • Click on "Enable Reposition" to reposition your profile picture.
  • Click "Save" to change your picture.
How do I upload or change my cover photo?
  • First go to your profile page
  • Hover over your Cover Photo and click on the "Options" icon on the right corner
  • Click on "Upload Image" to select a photo from your device
  • Click on "Browse Albums" to choose from your ringID photos
  • Click on "Enable Reposition" to reposition your cover photo
  • Click "Save" to change your cover photo
How to delete my profile picture/cover photo?
  • Click on the photos option from your ringID profile
  • You will see different categories of photo uploads
  • Click on profile pictures album
  • Click on the cross (X) on the photo you wish to delete
  • Your photo will be deleted
How do people view my profile? Is it different from how I view it?
The Feed is shown exactly how you would want others to view it. But you can personalize it using the privacy and security settings.
Can I customize my profile and feed page?
Yes, you can customize your profile by adding personal details and you can customize the feed based on your likes and interests with the pointer option and timer provided with every feed post.
How to log out/sign out of my account?
  • Click on the profile picture thumbnail located at the far right corner of the webpage
  • Scroll down to the end of the menu with the option "Logout"
FRIENDS

SECRET CHAT
How do I send a Secret Chat?
  • While chatting, click on the "Secret Chat" icon to turn Secret Chat ON or OFF.
  • After it is turned ON, the timer will be enabled where your desired time in seconds can be selected. Then, you can type your message and send it.
  • After your set time is over, the message will simply disappear and your chat will remain secret.
Is my Secret Chat stored somewhere?
No. Your secret chat disappears after it is viewed.
How do I delete the Secret Chat from my own chat screen?
  • Hover over your message and a 3 dot icon will appear
  • Click on "Delete" to remove secret chat from your chat screen
If someone deletes me from ringID, will they still have saved messages from secret chat?
No! The messages or images sent via secret chat automatically vanish after they have been viewed for the chosen time.

MEDIA CLOUD
What is Media Cloud?
Media Cloud is the ringID users’ very own music & media management system. ringID filters out regular posts and only keeps music & media content that you like, follow and are interested in separately in your Media Cloud. You can Discover various media content providers and Follow them to get regular posts on your feed. You can also Save media posts to be viewed later or download media to be viewed offline. Music and videos viewed while browsing ringID is automatically added to a recently viewed playlist allowing you to easily find them.
What do I do with the Discover option?
Discover shows you all the media sources available for you to follow on ringID. You can find and search for the latest music on top charts, upcoming artists and album reviews. You will find a search bar that helps you categorize and search media from around the world.
How do I create an album?
You can create albums on Media Cloud for organizing all your favorite music.
  • Click on the options (3 dot icon) on the bottom right of the media file you like and wish to add
  • Click on "Add to album"
  • You can either save the file on an existing album or create a new album
  • Click on "+Create new album"
  • Enter a name for your album
  • Click "Save"
How to delete an album?
  • Click on "Album" tab from media cloud
  • Click on either "Music/Videos" you wish to delete albums from
  • Click on the pointer on the album (located on upper right)
  • Click on "Remove album" option
How do I add a media file to my media cloud album?
  • Click on "Options" (3 dot icon) on the bottom right of the media file you want to add
  • Click on "Add to album"
  • Click "Add"
What does the playlist consist of?
Playlist consists of all the media files that you have played (both audio & video) from your feed allowing you to easily find them in one convenient playlist.

What is LIVE and Where can I find it?
LIVE is an exciting new feature which allows you broadcast live to your friends and the public. You can also view live broadcasts by others all over the world. You can call the live broadcaster to have a direct video call as well. Live is located on the right menu panel below the home icon.
What is the importance of broadcaster level and star broadcaster?
Level is a feature of ringID LIVE that determines the popularity of a broadcaster. With higher levels you will get more access to other exclusive features to improve your live broadcasts.
How to upgrade broadcaster level?
Level is determined by number of Coins & Votes.
  • Per 100,000 Coins = 1 Level
  • Per 2,000 Votes = 1 Level
How to become star broadcaster?
Star is determined by number of Coins.
  • Per 100,000 coins = 1 Star

  • Select the "Report" options to report the user
What is the difference between channel and LIVE?
Live is content broadcasted in real time and channel content is pre-recorded, not instant or in real time.
Why is Live not working?
This is highly irregular however may be due to issues related to internet connection. Try the following possible options:
  • Check your internet connection
  • Contact your ISP for solution, if needed
  • Make sure you are in close proximity of your router if you are using Wi-Fi
Upon establishing a valid internet connection, you may try restarting the app for regular service.

MEDIA CLOUD
What is Media Cloud?
Media Cloud is the ringID users very own music & media management system. ringID filters out regular posts and only keeps music & media content that you like, follow and are interested in separately in your Media Cloud. You can Discover various media content providers and Follow them to get regular posts on your feed. You can also Save media posts to be viewed later or download media to be viewed offline. Music and videos viewed while browsing ringID is automatically added to a recently viewed playlist allowing you to easily find them.
What do I do with the Discover option?
Discover shows you all the media sources available for you to follow on ringID. You can find and search for the latest music on top charts, upcoming artists and album reviews. You will find a search bar that helps you categorize and search media from around the world.
How do I create an album?
You can create albums on Media Cloud for organizing all your favorite music.
  • Tap on the options (3 dot icon) on the bottom right of the media file you like and wish to add
  • Tap on "Add to album"
  • You can either save the file on an existing album or create a new album
  • Tap on "+Create new album"
  • Enter a name for your album
  • Tap "Save"
How to delete an album?
  • Tap on "Album" tab from media cloud
  • Tap on the album you want to delete (Default Music/Video albums cannot be deleted)
  • Tap on the settings icon on the album (located on upper right)
  • Tap on "Delete Album" option
How do I add a media file to my media cloud album?
  • Tap on "Options" (3 dot icon) on the bottom right of the media file you want to add
  • Tap on "Add to album"
  • Tap "Add"
What does the playlist consist of?
Playlist consists of all the media files that you have played (both audio & video) from your feed allowing you to easily find them in one convenient playlist.
