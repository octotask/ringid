FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS (FAQ'S)

 
Registration

Registration is not available for Web Users. however, existing users are free to log in to web using their registered ringID.

Profile
How do I enter to my profile page?
You can enter your profile page by clicking on your profile picture which is always visible on the top left corner. Your name, ringID and online status are also visible at all times.
How to edit my Basic Info?
  • To edit your Basic Info go to your profile page.
  • Click on the "About" thumbnail from your profile.
  • Select your desired field such as Basic Info, Education, Professional Career, etc. and click on "Edit" to add/edit your information.
  • Click "Update" (web) or "Save" (desktop) to save your changes.
How do I upload or change my Profile Picture?
  • First go to your profile page.
  • Hover over your Profile Picture and click on the camera icon.
  • Click on "Upload Image" to select a photo from your device.
  • Click on "Browse Albums" to choose from your ringID photos.
  • Click "Save" to change your picture.
  • Click on "Enable Reposition" to reposition your profile picture.
How do I upload or change my Cover Photo?
  • First go to your profile page
  • Hover over your Cover Photo and click on the "Options" icon on the right corner
  • Click on "Upload Image" to select a photo from your device
  • Click on "Browse Albums" to choose from your ringID photos
  • Click "Save" to change your picture.
  • Click on "Enable Reposition" to reposition your profile picture
CALL

Calling is not available for Web Users.

Chat
How do I make a call to my friend in Chat?
Calling is not available for Web Users.
  • In the chat screen, there are multiple options displayed on the top panel
  • Click on the "Voice Call" icon to place a voice call
  • Click on the "Video Call" icon to place a voice call
How do I send stickers?
  • Click on the "Emoticon" icon to see your sticker packs
  • Click on the "Add" icon to download more sticker packs
  • Click on your desired sticker to send it
How many stickers are there to have fun with?
We are adding tons of stickers day and night. Virtually there are unlimited numbers of stickers for you to choose from.
How do I delete stickers from a conversation?
  • Hover over your sticker and a cross will appear on the top right corner
  • Click on the cross to delete the sticker
Secret Chat
How do I send a Secret Chat?
  • While chatting, click on the "Secret Chat" icon to turn Secret Chat ON or OFF.
  • After it is turned ON, the timer will be enabled where your desired time in seconds can be selected. Then, you can type your message and send it.
  • After your set time is over, the message will simply disappear and your chat will remain secret.
Is my Secret Chat stored somewhere?
No. Your secret chat disappears after it is viewed.
How do I delete the Secret Chat from my own chat screen?
  • Hover over your message and a cross will appear on the top right corner
  • Click on the cross to delete the message
If someone deletes me from ringID, will they still have saved messages?
No! The messages or images sent via secret chat automatically vanish after they have been viewed for the chosen time.
Friends
How do I search others in Friends?
  • Go to your profile page
  • Click on the "Friends" thumbnail from your profile
  • Select your desired friend from the list of your total friends
How to see the status of requests to other ringID Users?
  • Click on "Add Friends" icon on the left panel
  • Click on the "Pending" tab to get a list of your incoming and sent requests
How to invite people to ringID?
  • Click on "Add Friends" icon on the left panel
  • Click on the "Invite" tab to get a choice of invitation such as Facebook, Twitter, Email and Google Plus
How to delete any ringID users from my friendlist?
  • Go to the profile of the friend you want to delete
  • Click on Action and select "Unfriend" to delete the ringID users
Newsfeed
How do I write a post in ringID?
  • You can share your thoughts from the home screen by clicking on "Write Post"
  • You can share your thoughts with a status in your profile page by clicking on "Write Status"
  • You can tag your friends, share picture/music/video, share your location and much more
How do I edit my post?
  • To edit your post, click the pointer of the post you want to edit.
  • From the menu select the "Edit" option.
  • Make the necessary changes to your post.
  • Press "Post" to share the post on your Newsfeed.
How do I delete my post?
  • To delete your post tap the pointer of the post you want to delete.
  • From the menu select the "Delete" option to delete your post.
