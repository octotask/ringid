FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS (FAQ'S)

Registration
How do I register to ringID?
Registering to ringID is no hassle. You can easily register with ringID using any of the following:
  • Phone Number
  • Email Address
  • Facebook
  • Twitter
Using your phone number/email address, you will receive a code by a text message or by email. Simply enter the code to create your own ringID.
Using Facebook/twitter, you will get your own ringID using your Facebook/twitter login details on your device.
Is it safe to provide my Facebook or twitter details to get registered to ringID?
Yes! ringID does not require you to provide your login details for any other reason except, to register. This makes your sign up process easy and hassle free.
Profile
How do I enter to my profile page?
  • Tap the menu located on the top left corner of your home page.
  • Tap on "Profile" to open your profile page.
You can also go to your profile by tapping on your profile image.
How do I upload profile image?
  • First go to your profile.
  • Tap on your Profile Picture
  • You can upload a photo from your camera roll, from your ringID album, or capture a new photo from your phone camera.
How do I upload my Cover Photo?
  • First go to your profile.
  • Tap on your Cover Photo.
  • You can upload a photo from your camera roll, from your ringID album, or capture a new photo from your phone camera.
How to edit my Basic Info?
  • To edit your basic info go to your profile.
  • Select the "About" thumbnail from your profile and your basic information box will appear.
  • Tap "Edit" on your desired field such as Basic Info, Professional Career, Education, and more to add your information.
  • Tap "Done" to save your changes.
How to change my Cover Photo or Profile Picture?
  • Go to your profile.
  • Tap on the Cover Photo or the Profile Picture to change it.
  • You can upload a photo from your camera roll, from your ringID album, or capture a new photo from your phone camera.

Chat
How do I make a call to my friend in Chat?
  • Open the chat screen.

How do I send stickers?

How many stickers are there to have fun with?

How do I delete stickers from a conversation?

How do I Change the theme of the Chat?

Secret Chat
How do I send a Secret Chat?
  • When you start typing, in your normal chat box, you will find an option appear on the top "Secret Chat".
  • Tapping the button will allow you to turn Secret Chat ON or OFF.
  • After it is turned ON, the timer will be enabled where your desired time in seconds can be selected. Then, you can simply type your message and send it.
  • After your set time is over, the message will simply disappear and your chat will remain secret.
Is my Secret Chat stored somewhere?
No. Your secret chat disappears after it is viewed.
How do I delete the Secret Chat from my own chat screen?
  • Press and Hold the chat screen or the particular message for a short time.
  • Select the delete option to remove the message.
If someone deletes me from ringID, will they still have saved messages?
No! The messages or images sent via secret chat automatically vanish after they have been viewed for the chosen time.
Friends
How do I search others in Friends?
  • The "Friends" icon is located on the bottom menu bar of the home screen.
  • Tap the icon to open your list of Friends.
  • Tap the "Search" button to carry out a search.
How to see the status of requests to other ringID Users?
  • Open "Friends"
  • Tap "Add Friends"
  • The status of your requests is located in the "Pending" tab at the bottom.
  • A status in green arrow denotes a request sent to you from other ringID users.
  • A status in orange arrow represents a request sent by you to other ringID users.
How to invite people to ringID?
  • Open "Friends"
  • Tap "Add Friends"
  • A list of invitation medium such as Facebook, Twitter, Email etc. is provided inside the "Invite" tab.
  • Select your desired media.
How to delete any ringID users from my friendlist?
  • Go to the profile of the friend you want to delete.
  • Tap the ???Friend??? button.
  • Select ???Remove from friendlist??? to delete the friend.
