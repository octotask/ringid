Highlighted Features

All-in-one- enjoy a complete social experience through one single application. ringID doesn’t require user to install multiple apps, instead it came up with a light weight unified application for using its features.

Secret Chat- secrets do not remain secret if the other person can anticipate them in advance! ringID keeps the receiver ignorant of the arrival of a secret message by embedding the secret content in the same chat session. No new window, no different session, hence no chance of getting caught by a screen shot! Secret messages are embedded within the same chat window by setting a timer. Secret text messages simply disappear after the predefined time. Users can also can send secret images, which gets deleted within the set time. Sent secret audio and video can only be viewed once. User can simply turn off the secret chat option and continue to normal conversation.

Full authority of sent content- ringID lets chat logs to be deleted from both the sender’s and receiver’s ends upon the sender’s consent, thus providing you complete control over your sent content.

Unique ID- a unique ID is assigned to the user upon the registration. This unique ID lets the user to enjoy the flexibility of maintaining his/her account or contacts even if the device is lost.

Segmented Profile- everyone you socialize with is not a friend of yours. Benefit from segmented profile options to limit particular contacts to call & chat only while giving full profile access to your close ones.

Easy Sign Up- ringID does not require your phone number or email id to register you as a ringID user. Simply enter your name and a password to Sign Up!

Low on power and data- ringID keeps itself discreet in the background without hiking up your battery usage. Unique technology enables ringID to run low on your data usage on calls and chats.
