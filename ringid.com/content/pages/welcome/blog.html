The journey begins

While on a road trip to her in friend’s place in Nevada, Amelia was caught in dire straits when her car bumped on to a roadside tree trunk. The vehicle leaned on one side and moved across the roadside woodland and sled further in. Amelia got stuck between the steering wheel and her sit; she could barely move the lower half of her body. She did not find strong enough signals on her phone to dial out for help. There was Amelia, lost in the woods, alone, injured.

The courageous girl did not give up. She used ringID to send instant messages to her Circles and the responses were not late to come. Within half an hour a rescue team from the nearby police station was there in the spot and after a session of rescue effort for more than an hour, Amelia was finally there, under the open sky, grabbing some fresh air. What kept her on for the lonely periods? “I really cherished the warm tone of assurance of my husband on the other side of our ringID voice Call” said Amelia, “ringID kept going for me when the going got really tough!”
