Registration

The registration process is the procedure to verify and authorize a potential user’s identity into ringID.

ringID uses your mobile number as your identity hence it is required that you provide your mobile number during the installation process. For example, if your mobile number is 0123, then your unique identity on ringID will be ring0123.

Your mobile number is also required to verify your registration. ringID sends a passcode in your mobile number which you need to enter for completing your verification process.

We will never make your number visible to other users.

Please check your internet connection and your phone settings. If your internet connection is turned off then please turn it on and try to download again. If the problem persists then try the following steps:

  • Reboot your phone and try again to connect.
  • Upgrade ringID to the latest version: Press Download Now - once the download is complete, open the file to initiate installation.
  • Turn Wi-Fi off and back on (Settings > Wireless & networks > Wi-Fi)
  • Try connecting to different Wi-Fi hotspots.
  • Make sure that data is enabled (Settings > Wireless & networks > Mobile networks).
  • Contact your mobile provider for checking the validity of your data plan.

Upon opening ringID, you will require to register first to start experiencing the amazing features inside. ringID uses your phone number as your identity and upon submitting your number you will receive a passcode in the form of a SMS. You need to use this passcode as a verification to activate your ringID account. Once activated, you can start enjoying free calls, invisible messages, studio, mail and other features of ringID.

A passcode is a random code sent to you from ringID as a text message. The passcode is sent to you only after you have submitted your mobile number for registration.

Tap the "get passcode" button to get your passcode sent to you.

This code is needed to activate and verify your identity. By verifying the passcode ringID ensures that you are the original owner of the number provided ad someone else is not using your number in any unauthorized manner.

Please check you mobile network connections. The passcode might take longer time than normal in case of network disruptions.

The ringID registration process continues after the passcode has been entered. Information like your First Name, Last Name, Gender and Password are confirmed on this phase. The registration is completed once you select the Create account option.

Skipping the registration process will result in barring you from using the features of ringID. It is essential to complete the registration process to become a member of the vibrant ringID network where you will be able to connect and interact with fellow ringID friends.

In your message box you will find the timer icon on the top right corner in the shape of an hourglass. It becomes highlighted when it is turned on. You need to tap it once o turn it on. After turning it on you need to specify the amount of time you want the message to be displayed for. Now you can type your message and send it to the recipient. The message will be deleted once your set time has reached. You can also choose to type your message first and then turn the timer on.
