The journey begins

While on a road trip to her friend’s place in Nevada, Amelia was caught in dire straits when her car bumped on to a roadside tree trunk. The vehicle leaned on one side and moved across the roadside woodland & sled further in. Amelia got stuck between the steering wheel and her sit; she could barely move the lower half of her body. She did not find strong enough signals on her phone to dial out for help. There was Amelia, lost in the woods, alone, injured.

The courageous girl did not give up. She used ringID to send instant messages to her Circles and the response were not late to come. Within half an hour a rescue team from the nearby police station was there in the spot and after a session of rescue effort for more than an hour, Amelia was finally there, under the open sky, grabbing some fresh air. What kept her on during the lonely periods? "I really cherished the warm tone of assurance from my husband on the other side of our ringID voice Call" said Amelia, "ringID kept going for me when the going got really tough!"

While one might argue about the usefulness of social networking tools, ringID the ground breaking innovation in social networking, indeed played its part in a lifesaving event.

ringID lets its users make free calls and send instant messages to fellow ringID users. The voice clarity is undoubtedly among the best in the market and the instant messages are supported with extraordinary features like Secret Messages, Stickers etc.

Secret messages are really on demand these days since ringID introduced it. Users really enjoy secret chat with contacts which allows them to set a predefined time period for the sent message to be deleted once it has been viewed.

Furthermore, ringID also makes up for other niches of the social media with its features. ringID allows a user to share his or her stories with friends and also be updated by participating in the stories of others through its newsfeed. Users can upload content, comment, like and share posts.

ringID provides its users with a full-fledged "Studio" to work with. The "Studio" lets the user to create customized design contents for sharing with contacts.

There is an encrypted mailing service offered within the application. It works within the ringID servers to avoid interception for enhanced security and provides the user the convenience of not having to switch to a different mail application.
